About Me

Capturing the Unseen Beauty of Nature

Hello, I'm Bitan Hait, a passionate nature and macro photographer based in Dankuni, West Bengal, India. My journey in photography began as a way to connect with the natural world and reveal its hidden wonders.

Through my lens, I strive to showcase the intricate details and breathtaking beauty that often go unnoticed in our everyday lives. From the delicate patterns on a butterfly's wing to the complex structure of a leaf, I find endless inspiration in nature's smallest creations.

My work has been featured in several local exhibitions, and I continue to explore new techniques to capture the essence of the natural world. When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring the forests and fields of West Bengal, always searching for my next subject.
